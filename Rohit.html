Rohit Sharma

Full Name: Rohit Gurunath Sharma

Date of Birth: April 30, 1987

Place of Birth: Bansod, Nagpur, India

Role: Right-handed Batsman

Overview: Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer who is known for his exceptional batting skills, especially in limited overs cricket. He is the vice-captain of the Indian cricket team.

Career Highlights

  • Made his international debut in June 2007.
  • Sharma is the only player to have scored three double centuries in ODIs.
  • He holds several records for the highest individual scores in ODIs.
  • Played a key role in India's victories in various ICC tournaments.

Career and annual awards:

  • ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Year: 2019.
  • ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Year: 2019.
  • ICC Men's ODI Team of the Year: 2014 (12th man), 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019.
  • ICC Men's ODI Team of the Decade: 2011–2020.
  • ICC Men's T20I Team of the Decade: 2011–2020.
  • ICC Men's Test Team of the Year: 2021.
