Suryakumar Yadav

Full Name: Suryakumar Ashok Yadav

Date of Birth: September 14, 1990

Place of Birth: Mumbai, India

Role: Right-handed Batsman

Overview: Suryakumar Yadav is an Indian cricketer known for his aggressive batting style. He has represented Mumbai in domestic cricket and plays for the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Career Highlights

  • He made his First-Class debut for Mumbai in 2010.
  • Yadav has been a consistent performer in domestic cricket and the IPL.
  • He made his international debut for India in T20I cricket in 2021.
  • Suryakumar Yadav is known for his versatile batting and has potential for a successful international career.

Career and annual awards:

  • Being ranked as ICC No.1 T20 batsman and awarded the Mens Cricketer of the Year 2022.
  • he ended up being one of the most valuable players in IPL 2023.
  • After Kieron Pollard's retirement, He was made the Vice Captain of Mumbai Indians in IPL 2023.
  • He scored his maiden IPL century against Gujarat Titans on 12th May, 2023.
